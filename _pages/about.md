## Short Bio

I’m the founding Product & Engineering leader at **Exploris Health**, where I build **AI diagnostics** and **clinical foundation-model approaches** in cardiovascular disease. My scope spans **product definition & positioning**, **R&D**, and **clinical development / evidence generation**—from early strategy through real-world deployment.

Previously, I was a Director at **Idorsia**, leading **Real-World Evidence**, **HTA Statistics**, and a **Computational Science** group supporting both development and commercialization. Before that, I served as **Global Product & Machine Learning Leader** at **IBM**, responsible for the **Life Sciences portfolio** within IBM’s Global AI organization. I began my career as an **Equity Structurer** at **Deutsche Bank**, building quantitative models in high-pressure, decision-critical environments.
