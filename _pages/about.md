# Defining and Scaling AI Products in Precision Medicine

I am a product, engineering, and evidence leader building AI-driven diagnostics and therapeutic-enabling platforms. I specialize in **turning machine learning and biological signal into clearly defined, regulated medical products** — aligning **product strategy, evidence generation (RWE/clinical studies), regulatory pathways, and high-performing ML/engineering teams**.

**I’m particularly relevant for:** AI-biotech founders, VCs, and executive recruiters looking for an operator who can **translate an AI platform into programs and products** (diagnostics, companion diagnostics, therapeutic evidence generation) and scale the organization responsibly.

---

## What I Do (in practice)

### Product Definition in Regulated AI
- Define the product: clinical value proposition, user, workflow, and decision impact  
- Translate “platform” capabilities into concrete product roadmaps and program milestones  
- Shape build-vs-buy decisions, architecture, and operating model to match regulatory reality  

### Evidence Architecture (Clinical + Real-World)
- Design retrospective → prospective validation strategies and evidence roadmaps  
- Build RWE programs that support clinical adoption, HTA, and post-market requirements  
- Align endpoints and success metrics with regulatory expectations and therapeutic strategy  

### Building High-Performance Teams
- Build and lead ML + engineering organizations (hiring, structure, culture, delivery)  
- Establish execution discipline (reproducibility, documentation, testing, CI/CD) without slowing innovation  
- Align cross-functional teams across ML, clinical, regulatory, and product

## Short Bio

I am the founding Product and Engineering leader at **Exploris Health**, where I design and build **clinical foundation models** and **AI diagnostics** for cardiovascular disease. My scope spans **product definition & positioning**, **clinical development (evidence generation)**, and **R&D of foundation-model approaches**.

Previously, I was a Director at **[Idorsia](https://www.idorsia.com)**, leading **Real-World Evidence**, **HTA Statistics**, and a **Computational Science** group supporting both development and commercialization. Before that, I was the **Global Product & Machine Learning Leader** at **[IBM](https://www.ibm.com)**, responsible for the **Life Sciences portfolio** within IBM’s Global AI Team. I began my career as an **Equity Structurer** at **[Deutsche Bank](https://www.db.com)**, developing quantitative models in high-pressure, decision-critical environments.

## Research & Innovation (selected themes)

My research focuses on **representation learning** for clinical and physiological data, with emphasis on **safety, grounding, and translational utility**. Selected themes include:

- Learning from noisy, biased, incomplete real-world clinical data  
- Guiding transformers toward **[physiologically meaningful behavior](https://patents.google.com/patent/US20230092186A1/en)**  
- Biomarker discovery and multimodal disease modeling  
- Diagnostic accuracy and treatment-effect estimation  
- Applying language models to learn new **[disease representations (ontologies)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s43856-024-00698-2)**  

I focus on bridging foundational methods with deployable, clinically trustworthy AI systems — and on designing the evidence required to make them real in regulated environments.
